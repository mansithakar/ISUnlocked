Looking for someone to review your talk?

ISUnlocked provides a free service to the community pairing new speakers with conference veterans. Someone from our review board will spend some time with you to work on your content and presentation materials. This is a great opportunity for new speakers to get feedback before submitting to conferences.


  1. Complete this form so we can learn more about you and the conference you're preparing for.
  2. We will reach out to you via the contact information you provide, pairing you with an experienced reviewer.
  3. You and your reviewer will look over your paper and presentation materials.
  4. We'll ask you for some feedback and you're good to go!
