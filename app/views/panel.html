CFP Discussion Panel Discussion

On February 1st, 2017 we will be hosting a live chat with several conference organizers talking about some of the questions that many first-time speakers have. If you've never submitted to a conference and want to, then please join us to learn more. We will be sharing the link on our Twitter feed: @isunlocked.

Some of the topics we will discuss:

  • What is the review process like?
  • Should I submit my paper as early as possible?
  • Do you require that content must be brand new and ground-breaking?
