PARTY INFORMATION



This year at DEF CON InfoSec Unlocked will be hosting #ISUniversity in the Patrician Room of Caesar's Palace hosting CFP preparation workshops with industry veterans. We want to help new speakers to leave with a strong understanding of how to make a great CFP as well as help conference organizers facilitate more diverse and inclusive conferences.
