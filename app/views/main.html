What is ISUnlocked?

We are InfoSec Unlocked and we are an organization that supports diverse voices at computer security conferences.

Diversity & Outreach

We can help connect you to a fantastic network of groups dedicated to diversity within the many computer security conferences.

If you are a conference organizer and would like to learn more about diversity and inclusion please contact us contact us.

If you are looking to speak at conferences we have a variety of resources to get you started.

Submitting to a CFP?

If you are submitting to a CFP and would like someone to look over your paper, please register to have one of our reviewers look over it for you.

Someone from our team will contact you to pair you with a reviewer.

Speaking Opportunities

If you're looking to speak at a conference but do not know how to get started, we can help you with some of the basic first steps.

We will have a calendar of the major timelines for the conferences and links to get you started.

Feel free to reach out to us at anytime with any questions you have regarding our organization or submitting to conferences.

How can you help?

If you are interested in being a member of our conversations on diversity in information security, please shoot us an e-mail. We are looking for forum moderators, community organizers, conference reviewers, community partners, web page reviewers, data entry, calendar maintainers, etc.

We are also looking for people to join our CFP Review Board. E-mail t@tarah.org for more information about joining.

You can also follow us on Twitter to stay connected!
