Milestones

DEFCONcierge

Every year a welcome wagon for diversity in information security should occur at DEFCON, the largest InfoSec conference in the US. DEFCONcierge is a table near registration hosted by ISUnlocked in which we will be helping new con goers learn about all of the various events of the CON.

Leaders from villages and other organizations will frequently visit the DEFCONierge table to gather people to bring to their respective areas, this not only helps increase foot traffic at events but also provides a way for new people to get acclimated to the convention.
