Our Mission

Opening Doors for Diverse Voices in Information Security Conferences.
Connecting diverse groups of people in the infosec community through outreach.

One-Year Plan

  • DEF CON 25 Lecture Series
  • Connect with other diversity-oriented organizations
  • Create a best practices guide for organizing conferences
  • Become a 501c3 non-profit

Five-Year Plan

  • Aggregate stories from popular diverse voices to discuss their first times speaking
  • Increase the number of talks from diverse voices at confernces
  • Make it easier for members of underrepresented groups attend and speak at conferences via scholarships

Ten-Year Plan

  • Create a diverse community of conference speakers in information security
  • Give diverse voices a solid platform from which to start their career in speaking at conferences

Organization Roles


Executive Director
Current: AdaZebra

Executive Director of InfoSec Unlocked, making sure that conference events happen and helping organize volunteer efforts. Executive Director work with Incorporation and business keeping including, but not limited to banking, donations, and state and federal paperwork. Milestones


DEFCON Lecture Events

Every year a lecture series on how to create a CFP should occur at DEFCON, the largest InfoSec conference in the US. This can include lectures on how to come up with an idea, how to do research, how to write an appropriate paper, and how to make a good talk.


Conference Best Practices

Coming up with resources and information to help new and existing conferences to become more inclusive. This would include things like; how to build a Code of Conduct and why, how to handle sensitive incidents at your conference, how to make your conference more attractive and proactively recruit underrepresented speakers, etc.


Video Series
Current: Adazebra, Steve Christey Coley, Tyler Walker

This will include the current videos on diversity in information security and infosec conferences and a new upcoming series on how to approach call for papers. This new section will include 4-5 minute quick videos on how to brainstorm an idea, how to find the right conference, how to write a technical paper, how to build a presentation and how to give a compelling talk.
