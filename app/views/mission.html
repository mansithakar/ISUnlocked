Mission Statement

General Statement

Opening Doors for Diverse Voices in Security

Specific Aims

Help new voices be heard at information security conferences and connect with diversity organizations to provide a unified front.


Goals

One Year Plan

  • DEFCONcierge
  • Connect with other diversity-oriented organizations
  • Forum to discuss infosec conferences, promote voices at coferences and connect attendees
  • Become more organized as a group. More cohesive front to the topic

Five Year Plan

  • Bring together stories from popular diverse voices in the industry to discuss their first talks, jobs and expereinces
  • Increase the number of talks from diverse voices at specific conferences by convincing influential people at those conferences to help

Ten Year Plan

  • Bring together a diversity community in information security
