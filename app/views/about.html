Milestones Defined Projects

DEFCONcierge

Every year a welcome wagon for diversity in information security should occur at DEFCON, the largest InfoSec conference in the US. DEFCONcierge is a table near registration hosted by ISUnlocked in which we will be helping new con goers learn about all of the various events of the CON.

Leaders from villages and other organizations will frequently visit the DEFCONierge table to gather people to bring to their respective areas, this not only helps increase foot traffic at events but also provides a way for new people to get acclimated to the convention.

Past Activities What We've Done

DEF CON 23 Party

On Friday evening, August 7, 2015, we held an ISUnlocked party at DEF CON 23 in Las Vegas, NV. @Tarah, @AdaZebra and the Infosec Unlocked crew hosted, with music by DJ Jackalope. We had some great conversations and made some new friends in the process.


CFP Panel Discussion

On Wednesday, February 1st, 2017, we hosted a panel discussion centered around CFPs for smaller conferences. The panel was hosted by ISUnlocked founder @AdaZebra with panelists from BSIDES Boise, Dakotacon, BSIDES NOVA, and CircleCityCon.
